About Us

TIRADORESS (EH) is a powerful and highly respected criminal organization operating in the streets of Emergency Hamburg. Founded on the principles of loyalty, respect, and strength, we have grown into one of the most dominant gangs in the city.

We stand together as a family — protecting our members, controlling territory, and executing high-level operations.

Respect • Loyalty • Power • Unity
Once you join TIRADORESS, you become part of the family for life.
